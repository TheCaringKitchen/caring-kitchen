Putting It All Together

Coconut Curry & Rice

After cooking an extra large batch of tofu curry, I used the Muffin-Tin Method to save the leftovers.

The next time I had it, I added a small portion of shredded carrots and of rice – both of which had been frozen into mini-muffin tins to easily mix and match. It heated easily, had a great texture, and saved $30 on take-out.

This dish wasn't made from a recipe but it was inpsired by recipes like this: Tofu Coconut Curry.
