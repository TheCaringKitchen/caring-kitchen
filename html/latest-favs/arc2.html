Sausage Egg Bites

I used the Casserole-Dish Method to make these delicious sausage egg bites. There are endless ways to make them and here's a great recipe to start with: Cottage Cheese Egg Bake.

Cut and individually wrap before freezing.

From frozen, the microwave heats them through in under 2 min. Great breakfast on the go or quick snack to quell the hanger anytime.
