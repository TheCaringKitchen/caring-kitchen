Frozen Whipped Cream

I did not believe it at first, but it’s true - you can freeze whipped cream and thaw it without damaging the consistency. I went with the classic 2 tbs of sugar to 1 cup of cream and a dash of vanilla. At half price, this item will always be added to my grocery cart. Next time I’ll try putting some in ice cube trays for savory dishes. But I’ll keep doing the sweet, whipped cream too because it’s fun!
