Carrot Coconut Curry

This delisiously filling soup was inspired by Boulder Locavore's Curried Roasted Carrot Ginger Soup. It freezes and reheats well. Freezing in smaller muffin tins makes it easy to heat up in any size mug. Makes a comforting breakfast on the go.
