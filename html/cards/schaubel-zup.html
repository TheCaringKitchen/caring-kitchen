Grüna Schaubel Zup

This soup has been handed down through many Mennonite generations and everyone has a slightly different version. As long as there are green beans, onions, and summer savory, it will have the classic comfort. I like cooking up a big pot of it and then eating it morning, noon, and night for a few days. I usually add a dollop of sour cream, but some people add cream, and others don’t add anything - it’s perfect any which way. Here, Mennonite Girls Can Cook have written out a proper recipe.
