Roasted Red Soup

These roasted veggie soups turn out a bit different every time. The local produce was on for next to nothing, so I made a lot of this, and it turned out beautifully. I cut enough to fit the trays, covered it all in oil with a sprinkling of salt, baked at 400 degrees until it smelt delicious, blended with veggie stock, added dry cottage cheese for protein, and seasoned to taste. Water could be used as well; it would just take a bit more seasoning to taste. For a more precise direction, check out Clean Food Crush.
